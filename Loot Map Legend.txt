Legend:
I. Red dotted lines are guard paths
II. Black lines are Walls or obstruction
III. Grey lines on black lines are doorways or entrances
IV. Diamonds are player spawn points
V. White areas do not contain loot



1. House Loot Layout Legend

	A. Green are bedrooms, these can very rarely spawn firearms or car keys for extra money but any sound made will alert the police
	B. Blue Is Kitchen and dinning room, spawn kitchen items e.g. pots, pans, plates, and rarely, Fine chinaware and Vases
	C. Yellow is an office, it can spawn laptops, phones, watches, and rarely car keys
	D. Orange is the garage, it is the player's spawn area so does not spawn loot
	E. Brown is the Living room area, can spawn vases, and small lamps

2. Office Loot Layout Legend

	A. Green are offices that contain higher value 'office items'
	B. Blue lines are cubical dividers, these are smaller than walls
	C. Yellow boxes are desks that contain less overall office loot
	D. The grey box in the center is a feature/half-wall to block guard vision
